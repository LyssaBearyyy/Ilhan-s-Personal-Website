ABOUT MYSELF

Assalamualaikum, and greeting to all of my followers! My name is Ilhan Danial Bin Mohammad Salikin, and I am 22 years old. I am from Kuching, Sarawak, and I am the elder of two siblings, with a younger sister.

I graduated with a diploma in civil engineering from Politeknik Kuching Sarawak, where I learned civic engineering, cooperation, and solving issues. I currently work as an assistant manager at Sarawak Consolidated Industries Berhad (Concrete Manufacturing Sdn. Bhd.), a position that requires me to perform well and contribute to my company's success. I am passionate about continually learning and hope to progress both professionally and personally by accepting new opportunities and responsibilities.

I have a range of hobbies outside of work that help me stay inspired and motivated. I love playing games, and in my free time, I like to play both competitive and narrative games. I also have a deep affection for cats and enjoy taking care of and spending time with my lovely cats

Collecting and building Gundam models is one of my favorite hobbies because the process involves patience, creativity, and perfection. This is a very enjoyable and therapeutic hobby for me. Another way I express myself is through my love of music, particularly when I'm playing the guitar. I like learning new songs, playing with melodies, and improving my skills because music is a my therapeutic release.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a little better. I’m looking forward to sharing more about my journey, interests, and experiences with you all in the Future.
